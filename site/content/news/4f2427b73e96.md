China’s AI models have Trump’s AI world at war with itself
Last weekend, several current and former advisers to President Donald Trump on AI publicly lobbed insults at the country’s leading AI companies.

Every time a new smart, free model from China gets released, US companies see less reason to fork out money for models from Anthropic or OpenAI.

That’s creating economic and political problems for the president—and dividing the top AI strategists in his orbit.
