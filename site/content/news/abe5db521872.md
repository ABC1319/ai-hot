Can a handful of engineers really do the work of an army of consultants? That’s the bet behind Ode with Anthropic — the joint venture dedicated to embedding forward-deployed engineers in enterprise firms, backed by Anthropic, Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, Goldman Sachs and others.
On this episode of TechCrunch’s
Equity
podcast, Rebecca Bellan sits down with Ode’s leaders
Chris Taylor
and
Eddie Siegel,
who founded Fractional AI, the applied AI services startup that Ode
acquired earlier this year
to serve as the new venture’s core.

The three discuss why so many enterprise AI pilots never make it to production and why they think AI-native services are about to become one of the biggest categories in tech.
